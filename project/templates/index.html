About us

Your Peaceful Sanctuary Near Sacred Grounds

Where modern comfort meets spiritual tranquility, creating the perfect retreat for pilgrims and travelers on their sacred journey.

Located on the scenic Hyderabad–Vijayawada Highway and just steps away from the sacred Kanaka Durga Temple, Hotel Varahi Grand is your ideal boutique retreat that perfectly blends comfort, convenience, and affordability. Our 10 thoughtfully designed air-conditioned rooms offer modern amenities including TV, clean attached bathrooms, water heaters, and complimentary bottled water, ensuring a spotless and serene stay for pilgrims, families, and travelers alike. With our commitment to friendly service, pristine accommodations, and unbeatable location, HVG has become the trusted choice for guests seeking a genuine home-away-from-home experience where hospitality meets simplicity, making every stay both memorable and meaningful.
