Refund & Cancellation Policy

We are dedicated to ensuring your stay with us at Hotel Varahi Grand is comfortable and enjoyable. Please be advised that we operate under a strict No Refund Policy for the following reasons:

  1. Reservation Commitment: When you make a reservation with Hotel Varahi Grand, you are booking specific dates and room(s). These confirmed bookings may prevent us from accepting other potential guests. As such, we are unable to offer refunds for cancellations or no-shows.
  2. Operational Considerations: We allocate staff and resources based on confirmed reservations to maintain our service standards. Last-minute cancellations impact our ability to operate efficiently, which is why we strictly follow a no-refund policy.
  3. Fair Treatment for All Guests: To ensure consistency and fairness for every guest, our No Refund Policy is applied uniformly. This policy helps us maintain a balanced and dependable reservation system for everyone.
  4. Special Circumstances: In the event of an emergency or unforeseen circumstance, we encourage you to get in touch with our reservations team. While we do not offer refunds, we may review your case and consider offering a credit for future use at our discretion.

We appreciate your understanding and cooperation regarding our No Refund Policy. If you have any questions or require assistance, feel free to contact our team at reservations@varahigrand.com.

We look forward to welcoming you to Hotel Varahi Grand and ensuring a safe, comfortable, and memorable stay.
